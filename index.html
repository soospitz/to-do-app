Yosemite National Park

  • Yosemite is third national park but sparked the idea of national parks.
  • Yosemite has one of tallest waterfall in the world.
  • The name "Yosemite" (meaning "killer" in Miwok) originally referred to the name of a tribe which was driven out of the area (and possibly annihilated) by the Mariposa Battalion.
